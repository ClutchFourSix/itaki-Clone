FAQ

Keep objections low. Answer the obvious questions directly and without fluff.

Is this a full automated marketplace?

Not yet. This version is intentionally manual so we can validate demand and improve the offer before building more software.

Can I choose my coach?

Yes. You can request a coach directly, or we can match you based on your goal and your preferred style.

Do you help with general English too?

Yes, but the strongest offer is interview-focused coaching because it solves a painful, urgent, high-value problem.

What should the owner improve next?

Add a form, a payment link, real tutor photos, genuine testimonials, and eventually a booking calendar. But do not wait for those to start selling.
